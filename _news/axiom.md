**The Louisiana Board of Regents has awarded full funding** to establish the
**Applied eXploration in Open Mathematics Lab (AXIOM Lab)**—a dedicated collaboration space supporting mentored undergraduate research in open mathematics.

---

### Quick facts
- **Program:** Board of Regents Support Fund — Departmental Enhancement
- **Amount:** $60,875
- **Home:** Mathematics & Statistics, Louisiana Tech University
- **Focus:** Mentored undergraduate research, collaboration, and community-building

--- 

### What the AXIOM Lab will support
- A dedicated, student-centered research collaboration space
- Mentored undergraduate projects and course-based research experiences
- Workshops, seminar series, and research “on-ramp” activities
